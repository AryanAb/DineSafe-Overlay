DineSafe Overlay

Browser Extension for Toronto Dining

v0.1

🛈 Welcome

This extension fetches data from Toronto's DineSafe program and displays badges based on inspection data.

➕ Score Calculation

The scoring system penalizes significant infractions more than minor ones. It also penalizes more recent infractions more than past ones. The lower the points the better!

Failed Inspections

Establishments which failed their most recent inspection automatically receive maximum points.

Point System

  • Significant Infractions: 3 points
  • Other Infractions: 1 point

Multiplier System

For the most recent inspection, the multiplier is equal to 1.0. The multiplier is reduced by 0.2 for each previous multiplier until a minimum of 0.2.

🥇 Badges

Badges are assigned based on the final inspection score.

🟢 Score ≤ 3

Safe establishment to dine at

🟡 3 < Score ≤ 6.5

Mostly safe, but some infractions noted

🔴 Score > 6.5

Dining may come with some risks

❓ Unknown Score

The extension was not able to find info on the establishment
